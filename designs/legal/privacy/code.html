1. Introduction

Welcome to TrendStocker. Your privacy is critically important to us. At TrendStocker, we have a few fundamental principles: We don't ask you for personal information unless we truly need it. We don't share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law, develop our products, or protect our rights.

This Privacy Policy applies to our website and all services provided by TrendStocker. By accessing our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection

person Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you, including email addresses, name, and phone number.

devices Usage Data

We may also collect information on how the Service is accessed and used. This includes your IP address, browser type, browser version, and the pages of our Service that you visit.

3. Data Usage

lock Encryption
